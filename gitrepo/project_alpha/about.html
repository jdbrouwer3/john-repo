John Brouwer


@john_brouwer3

I believe that listening to music that you enjoy is very valuable. In addition, I think that listening to a variety of music genres helps listeners appreciate each genre more for what it does well. Jeniffer Nettles of Sugarland perhaps best describes country music by acknowleding why a lot of people don't like it - "They say, 'Oh, country music is about your dog dying and your truck breaking down and your wife cheating.' But there's a kernel of truth to that stereotype - and I love it. That is what country music does better than any other genre, in my opinion. It celebrates brokenness. And it celebrates realness in a way that helps us to heal."

This deep love for music goes back to my childhood when I would always have the radio on. In fact, at a young age, I frequently imagined working as a DJ at a country music station even though I was terrified of public speaking. At that time I thought it would be a fun job because it would not feel like real work.

Still today, listening and talking about music are still big hobbies in my life. I thoroughly enjoy finding new music to listen to weekly and want to share my love for music with the world!
