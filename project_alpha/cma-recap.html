CMA Awards Recap
It was Country Music's biggest night when all the stars got together for the 52th Annual CMA Awards in Nashville, Tennessee. Kicked off with a medly with Luke Bryan, Ashley McBride, Brothers Osborne, Brad Paisley, and Miranda Lambert, the night was filled with legendary performances with collaberations with Ricky Skaggs and Mavis Staples.
